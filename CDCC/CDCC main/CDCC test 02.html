Mission Statement

Reconizing that career development is a life long process, the mission of Career Development & Counseling Center (CDCC) is to guide and support students as they explore and further understand themselves,
evalute career options, gain valuable experience, develop post-graduation career plans, and achieve their full potentials.

Long Term Goals

  • Build a strong collaboration among SDA institutuins by creation goals and activities that are mutually beneficial to instiutions.
  • Provide information that enchanced student's interest and abilities through career exploration.
  • Provide guidance and support through internship and employment opportunities for students and alumni.
  • Provide updated and useful information related to scholarshops, volunteer work or community services, etc.
  • Establish network by seeking MoU and agreements.
  • Establish alumni network and build strong collaboration.
